What We Do

Vuka Darkie Manufacturing Stokvel was founded in August 2024 with a clear mission: to empower Black communities by pooling financial resources and investing in township-based manufacturing ventures.

Through our collective investment model, we're building sustainable wealth and creating jobs in our communities.

To promote and foster a culture of saving and investing funds together in order to achieve one common goal of collectively owning large scale means of production (machinery).

ABSA Stokvel Best Enablers Award 2024

In September 2024, Vuka Darkie Manufacturing Stokvel was honored with the prestigious ABSA Stokvel Best Enablers Award, recognizing our innovative approach to community investment and economic empowerment.

This award celebrates stokvels that go beyond traditional savings models to create lasting economic impact and enable community development.

The recognition from ABSA, one of South Africa's leading financial institutions, validates our model and has helped accelerate our growth and visibility.

"Vuka Darkie Manufacturing Stokvel represents the future of community investment—directing collective savings toward productive assets that create jobs and build sustainable wealth."
— ABSA Stokvel Awards Committee
