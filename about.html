About Us

Our Vision & Mission

Vision

To create a self-sustaining Black manufacturing ecosystem that generates intergenerational wealth and economic empowerment within township communities.

Mission

Empower Black communities by pooling financial resources and investing in township-based manufacturing ventures that create jobs, build skills, and establish economic independence.

Why Manufacturing? Why Stokvels?

Manufacturing Creates Real Value

Manufacturing is the backbone of economic development. By investing in manufacturing, we create tangible products, sustainable jobs, and develop valuable skills within our communities.

Stokvels: Our Cultural Strength

Stokvels are deeply rooted in our culture as a collective savings mechanism. We're leveraging this trusted community model and directing it toward productive investment rather than consumption.

Building Intergenerational Wealth

By pooling our resources and investing in productive assets, we're creating a foundation for sustainable wealth that can be passed down through generations.

Community Ownership

Our model ensures that manufacturing facilities are owned by the community they serve, keeping profits and economic benefits within our townships.
